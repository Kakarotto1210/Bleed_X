2. **Leak Detection Logic**: The backend runs deterministic thresholds against specific metrics: Subscriptions, Extreme Micro-Spends (<₹500 frequency), Circadian Logic (11PM - 3AM charges), and Capital Concentration.
3. **Data Federation**: Combines this into a JSON-persisted 'Terminal Payload' encompassing the user's `System Burn Limit` and an overall `Leak Score`.
4. **Action Protocols**: The Frontend Reacts. It displays Interactive Matrices where users can push limits, generate CSV reports, hook SMS gateways, and physically trigger "Cancel Subscriptions" protocols.
